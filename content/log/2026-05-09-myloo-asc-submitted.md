Build went up. Listing went up. Screenshots went up — iPhone 6.9", iPhone 6.5", iPad 13", all five editorial templates in their slots.

The status-bar pre-crop trick paid off. iPhone-frame mockups have a fake 9:41 status bar, and the actual app inside also renders one — so the doubled clock looks like a bug. Cropping the device frame's status bar before mounting the screenshot fixed it cleanly.

Now the queue. Apple takes 24-48h for first-time review on a new app. If they reject, fix and resubmit. If they approve, MyLoo goes live and we send the launch wave to the IBS subreddits.
